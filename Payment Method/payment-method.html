Payment Methods

Several payment methods to facilitate your purchase. Either through the website or in one of the physical stores, you can choose from different payment methods, so that you can pay in the way that suits you best. Below you can see which payment methods are available in the store. Pay with total security and confidence.

ATM Reference

  • The site will send an entity, a reference and the value of your order to your email, which you can settle at any ATM or Homebanking;
  • Orders with payment by ATM Reference are automatically canceled if they are not paid after 8 hours; Payments via ATM Reference do not need to send receipts;
  • The store recommends this payment method for online orders, due to its convenience and faster processes.

MBWAY

  • With MB WAY you can pay for your order using your mobile phone number associated with this service;
  • Orders paid by MB WAY are automatically canceled if they are not paid within 5 minutes;
  • Payments via MB WAY do not need to send receipts.

ATM card / VISA / MASTERCARD

  • In each of our stores, payment via bank card is available;
  • In each of our stores you can pay via ATM Card, VISA and Mastercard;
  • Payment via American Express not available.

Check

  • Payment will only be accepted by check up to a maximum amount of €150;
  • If you wish to pay an amount greater than €150 by check, the material will only be delivered upon receipt of payment;
  • Confirmation of the charge amount may take up to 5 business days.

3x 4x Oney

  • 3x 4x Oney is a personal loan that allows you to finance purchases made on partner sites. It's a simple, easy, secure and free way to pay for your online purchases, between €75 and €2,000, in 3 or 4 installments (without interest or associated charges). Just want, choose and buy;
  • When paying through x 4x Oney, it will not be allowed under any circumstances to change it - name, shipping or billing data.
  • To access the 3x 4x Oney solution, you must have a debit or credit bank card, Visa or Mastercard, issued by an institution authorized to operate in Portugal and valid for thirty days or more from the expiry of the term. chosen refund. Installment payments are exclusively made by debiting the bank card indicated by you;

Credit/Installments

  • You can pay using personal credit financing. To do so, you must select the credit option when placing the order;
  • Payment via credit available for orders equal to or greater than €250;
  • Credit subject to approval by Cetelem credit institution;
